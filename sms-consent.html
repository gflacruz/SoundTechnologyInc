SMS Consent Policy

Proof of Consent (Opt-In) Collected

Proof of Consent (Opt-In) Collected

Sound Technology Inc collects explicit consent from users before sending SMS/MMS communications. The following outlines the process by which consent is obtained, stored, and maintained in compliance with applicable regulations.

1. Consent Collection Methods

Sound Technology Inc obtains opt-in consent via the following methods:

  • Website Contact Forms: Customers provide explicit consent by entering their phone number and checking an opt-in box on our contact form that clearly states they agree to receive SMS/MMS messages from Sound Technology Inc.
  • In-Person Sign-Ups: Customers provide written or electronic consent when filling out service intake forms at our physical location.

2. Clear Disclosure & Messaging Compliance

At the point of consent, users are informed of:

  • The purpose of SMS/MMS communication (e.g., repair status updates, service notifications).
  • The frequency of messages (messages vary based on service status).
  • A statement that message and data rates may apply.
  • How to opt out (e.g., “Reply STOP to unsubscribe”).
  • Links to our Privacy Policy.

3. Consent Storage & Record-Keeping

Sound Technology Inc maintains records of user consent, including:

  • Timestamp of opt-in.
  • Source of opt-in (e.g., website form, in-store intake).
  • Message logs confirming consent request and user acknowledgment.

4. Opt-Out & Preference Management

Users may opt out at any time by:

  • Replying “STOP” to any SMS/MMS message received.
  • Contacting our support team at (813) 985-1120 or service@soundtechnologyinc.com to request removal.

Sound Technology Inc ensures that opt-out requests are processed promptly, and users will no longer receive SMS/MMS communications unless they opt in again.

5. Compliance Monitoring & Updates

Sound Technology Inc regularly reviews its SMS consent process to remain compliant with industry regulations.

For any questions regarding SMS opt-in compliance, please contact us at service@soundtechnologyinc.com.
